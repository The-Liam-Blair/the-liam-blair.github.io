Back to Roots

Short 2.5D horde shooter developed as part of the 2023 Global Game Jam.

Itch.io GitHub

Submission for the 2023 Global Game Jam (With the theme of "Roots"), Back to Roots is a short horde shooter about finding your family-turned "Haunted" from the woods and taking matters into your own hands.

Developed with a 5-man team, I was responsible for implementing the combat mechanics and visuals for the flamethrower, which was handled using a particle system. Initially starting out as just a simple square hitbox for the flamethrower's damage collider, it soon expanded to using the colliders on the flame particles to register damage events for better control of the weapon. Finally a damage-over-time effect was added, as well as flashy damage numbers, to make the combat visually more appealing and impactful.
